# ET Alpha — The Conviction Engine

**"From Noise → Signal → Decision → Action"**

A next-generation AI-powered stock market intelligence platform tailored for Indian retail investors. 
ET Alpha bridges the gap between data noise and confident market actions with a hybrid intelligence engine merging GenAI, predefined ML models, and high-frequency real-time signals.

---

## 🎯 Features

### 1. Signal Fusion Engine (The Brain)
- **Confluence Score Engine**: Synthesizes technical patterns, news sentiment, volume anomalies, momentum shifts, and smart money signals into a single 1-10 Conviction Score.
- **Multi-Signal Weighting**: Adjusts context dynamically (e.g., strong sentiment down-weights weak technicals); resolves conflicting signals while preventing bias.
- **Historical Confidence Layer**: Precomputed success rates, average returns, and duration for discovered market setups based on historical precedents.

### 2. Smart Money Intelligence (The Edge)
- **Institutional Simulation Engine**: Models behavior of Mutual Funds, FIIs, and Insiders to output Buy/Hold/Sell perspectives per entity.
- **Alignment & Gap Analysis**: Highlights mismatches between retail sentiment and institutional flows, measuring how closely your portfolio mimics "smart money".
- **Footprint Detector**: Traces accumulation zones, distribution phases, and sudden institutional volume entries.

### 3. Opportunity Radar (The Eyes)
- **Early Signal Detection**: Scans news feeds and volume spikes to identify opportunities before they hit mainstream media.
- **High-Conviction Alerts**: Triggers continuous streams of alerts (e.g., "Breakout + Sentiment + Volume anomaly detected").
- **Opportunity Ranking**: Curates signals continuously by confluence score and confidence level.

### 4. AI Reasoning Layer (The Mind)
- **Bull vs Bear Debate Agent**: A spectacular dual-agent system generating powerful competing arguments for any stock.
- **Verdict Agent**: Combines the debate with historical signals to forge a final stance and confidence percentage.
- **Explainability Engine**: Translates complex analysis into simple, plain-English justifications (including downside risks and invalidation conditions).

### 5. Behavioral Intelligence (Psycho Edge)
- **Retail Trap Detector**: Identifies FOMO zones, pump-and-dump patterns, and overhyped assets.
- **Crowd vs Smart Money Divergence**: Flags critical situations where retail investors are bullish but institutions are discreetly bearish.
- **Hype Cycle Positioning**: Classifies asset maturity (Early, Mid-Hype, Peak Hype, Exhaustion).

### 6. Portfolio Intelligence (The Mirror)
- **Snapshot & Impact Analyzer**: Reviews sector exposure, risk concentration, and simulates the impact of new additions.
- **Risk & Overexposure Detection**: Warns of excessive sector imbalances and provides instantaneous risk scoring.

### 7. Interaction Layer (The Interface)
- **Sensory & Visual Frontend**: Features scrolling storytelling, shiny gold/violet/monochrome palettes, and stunning 3D levitating geometry.
- **CLI & Natural Language Interaction**: Query specific assets (e.g., "Should I buy Infosys?") for instant decision output.
- **Alert Feed**: Real-time simulation of high-value market notifications.
